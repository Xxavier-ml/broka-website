How BROKA Works

Four simple steps to a fair deal. No stress, no scams, just smooth transactions.

1

List or Browse

Sellers list items with asking price. Buyers browse GPS-based listings nearby in their city or region.

2

Start Negotiation

Instead of haggling directly, both parties chat with BROKA — your impartial AI broker.

3

AI Finds Fair Price

BROKA analyzes live market data and suggests a win-win price both parties can genuinely agree on.

4

Secure Exchange

Pay via M-Pesa with full escrow protection. Funds release only when both sides confirm delivery.
