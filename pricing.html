Pricing FAQs

Everything you need to know about how BROKA charges.

When exactly is the fee charged?

+

The 3% fee is only charged when a deal is successfully confirmed by both parties and funds are released from escrow. If a negotiation fails or a deal falls through, you pay nothing.

Who pays the fee — buyer or seller?

+

By default, the fee is split between buyer and seller (1.5% each). However, sellers can choose to absorb the full 3% to attract more buyers, or buyers can cover it for premium listings. You decide.

Is there a minimum or maximum transaction amount?

+

Minimum transaction is KSh 500. There is no maximum — BROKA handles everything from phone accessories to vehicles and property. High-value transactions above KSh 5M may require identity verification.

Can I cancel a Pro or Business plan anytime?

+

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Your benefits last until the end of the billing period. Annual plans are non-refundable but transferable to another account.

Do you support currencies other than KSh?

+

Currently BROKA is optimized for KSh with M-Pesa. Support for UGX (Uganda), TZS (Tanzania), NGN (Nigeria), and GHS (Ghana) is on the roadmap for Q3 2026 as we expand across Africa.
