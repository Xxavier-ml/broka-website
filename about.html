Our Story

We're Building the Fairest Marketplace in Africa

BROKA was born out of frustration — with scams, with unfair deals, with the anxiety of not knowing if you're paying too much or selling too low. We decided to fix it with AI.

Our Mission

Fair Deals for Every African

Across Africa, millions of everyday transactions happen in an environment of information asymmetry. Sellers don't know the true market value. Buyers can't tell if they're being overcharged. And both parties carry the risk of being scammed.

BROKA changes this equation by introducing an impartial AI broker — powered by Llama 3.3 70B — into every transaction. Our AI knows market prices. It mediates without taking sides. It protects both buyer and seller with escrow. And it does all of this for just 3%.

We started in Kenya because we know Kenya. But BROKA is built for Africa.

The Problem We Solve

African Marketplaces Are Broken

Here's what happens every day on existing platforms — and how BROKA fixes it.

😰

Blind Haggling

Neither side knows the real market price. Sellers overprice out of fear, buyers underbid out of mistrust.

🚨

Rampant Scams

Fake listings, advance fee fraud, and payment ghosting are normalised on current platforms.

😤

Awkward Negotiations

Direct negotiation is stressful. One wrong message and a deal dies — or a relationship gets ruined.

↓

✓ Market Intelligence

BROKA pulls real-time comparable prices so both parties negotiate from fact, not guesswork.

✓ Escrow Protection

Funds are held securely until both parties confirm satisfaction. No payment until you're happy.

✓ AI-Mediated Deals

BROKA handles the negotiation so you don't have to. Polite, data-driven, always fair.
