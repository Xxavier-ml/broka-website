Privacy Policy

🔒 BROKA does not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, phone number, location, listing details) and information generated by your use of the platform (transaction history, negotiation logs, device identifiers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the AI broker and generate fair price recommendations
  • To process payments and hold funds in escrow
  • To detect and prevent fraud
  • To send transactional communications (deal confirmations, escrow releases)
  • To improve our AI models (anonymised, aggregated data only)

3. Data Sharing

We share data with payment processors (M-Pesa / Safaricom) strictly for transaction processing, and with identity verification partners for fraud prevention. We do not share data with advertisers.

4. Data Retention

Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Kenyan financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account and non-transactional data at any time via privacy@broka.africa.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, port, and request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@broka.africa to exercise these rights.

Escrow Terms

🔒 Escrow protection is included in every BROKA transaction at no extra cost. Funds are held securely until both parties confirm satisfaction.

1. How Escrow Works

When a deal is reached via AI negotiation or auction, the buyer's funds are transferred to BROKA's escrow account via M-Pesa. Funds are held until the buyer confirms receipt and satisfaction with the item or service.

2. Release Conditions

Funds are released to the seller automatically when: (a) the buyer confirms deal satisfaction in-app, or (b) 48 hours pass after confirmed delivery with no dispute raised.

3. Refund Conditions

Funds are returned to the buyer when: (a) the seller cancels the deal, (b) the item is not as described and a dispute is upheld, or (c) the seller fails to deliver within the agreed timeframe.

4. Dispute Window

Buyers have 48 hours after confirmed delivery to raise a dispute. After this window, funds are automatically released to the seller. For high-value transactions (above KSh 100,000) the window extends to 72 hours.

5. Coverage Limit

Standard escrow protection covers transactions up to KSh 500,000. Transactions above this amount require additional identity verification and may be subject to manual review.

6. Prohibited Escrow Bypass

Attempting to negotiate payment outside the BROKA escrow system (e.g. requesting direct M-Pesa outside the app) is a violation of terms and will result in account suspension.
